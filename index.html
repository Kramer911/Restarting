The Center is committed to assist in the access of resources, information and services.


It also helps to create opportunities for individuals in the Autism Spectrum, their families, caregivers, community members and professionals.

We help provide access to community resources or services and encourage the development of maximum independence for individuals with Autism Spectrum Disorder. This includes relationship building and social experiences between people with Autism Spectrum Disorder, their families, and peers.

After a time of transition, the center tries to develop new programs and services while working hard trying to meet the needs of people on the spectrum and those that might be affected by it.

All of our programs and services will fall within four main streams of focus!

  • Awareness
  • Education and training
  • Resources and Referrals
  • Support and Socialization
